6. BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS: FEASIBILITY RISK DIAGNOSTIC

Break-even identifies the minimum production volume to cover all costs without profit or loss. It is a critical risk indicator.

6.1 Break-Even Calculation

Formula: BEP (units/month) = Fixed Costs ÷ Contribution Margin per Unit

Where: Contribution Margin per Unit = Selling Price − Variable Cost per Unit

Calculation:

Contribution Margin = BDT 12.00 − BDT 8.90 = BDT 3.10/brick

BEP = BDT 1,85,000 ÷ BDT 3.10 = 59,677 bricks/month
